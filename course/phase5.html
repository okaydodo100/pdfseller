Lesson 2

Method 1: Reddit (Highest ROI Free Traffic)

Reddit is a goldmine for targeted traffic. Your buyers are literally asking questions you can answer.

The Reddit Strategy:

Step 1: Find 5-10 subreddits where your audience asks questions

You already did this research in Phase 1. Now you're going back to help.

Step 2: Answer questions with genuinely helpful responses

Spend 20-30 minutes writing a detailed, valuable answer. Don't mention your product yet.

Step 3: Build karma and credibility (1-2 weeks)

Post helpful comments. Become a recognized helpful member.

Step 4: Mention your product when genuinely relevant

"I actually created a guide on this exact thing..." ‚Äî only when it actually helps.

Lesson 3

Method 2: Facebook Groups

Facebook Groups are full of people asking for help with exactly what you teach.

The Facebook Group Strategy:

Step 1: Join 10-20 groups in your niche

Look for active groups with 10K+ members. Mix of large and medium-sized.

Step 2: Engage genuinely for 1 week

Comment on posts. Answer questions. Be helpful without any agenda.

Step 3: Create value posts

Share a tip, insight, or mini-guide. Something genuinely useful. No pitch.

Step 4: When people ask for more...

"I have a complete guide on this ‚Äî DM me if you want the link." Move to DMs.

Daily Routine (30 min/day):

  • ‚Üí 10 min: Scroll and answer 3-5 questions helpfully
  • ‚Üí 10 min: Engage with other people's posts (comments, reactions)
  • ‚Üí 10 min: Create one value post in one group
Lesson 4

Method 3: Quora

Quora answers rank in Google. One good answer can send traffic for years.

The Quora Strategy:

Step 1: Search your topic keywords

Find questions with high views but mediocre answers.

Step 2: Write the BEST answer on the platform

500-1000 words. Detailed. Formatted well. Include everything they need.

Step 3: Add a soft CTA at the end

"If you want the complete system, I put together a guide here: [link]"

Step 4: Answer 2-3 questions per day

Consistency compounds. After 30 days, you'll have 60-90 answers driving traffic.

Method 5: YouTube Comments (Underrated)

Big YouTubers in your niche already have your audience. Their comment sections are goldmines.

The Strategy:

  1. 1. Find 10-20 YouTube channels in your niche

  3. 3. When they post, be one of the FIRST to comment
  4. 4. Leave a genuinely valuable comment (not "great video!")

Lesson 8

Getting Your First Sale (The Push)

Want to accelerate your first sale? Here's the fast-track method:

The "First 10 Customers" Method:

  1. 1. Make a list of 50 people who might benefit from your product. Friends, family, colleagues, online connections.
  2. 2. Reach out personally ‚Äî not with a pitch, but asking for feedback. "Hey, I created this guide about [topic]. Would you mind taking a look and telling me what you think?"
  3. 3. Offer a discount for early supporters. "Since you helped me test it, here's 50% off if you want the full version."
  4. 4. Ask for referrals ‚Äî "Know anyone else who struggles with [problem]? I'd love to help them too."

This isn't scalable, but it gets you your first reviews, testimonials, and proof of concept.

Offer 30-50% commission to people who promote your product.
