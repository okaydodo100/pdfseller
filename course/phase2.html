Lesson 7

The Iteration Process

Claude rarely gets it perfect on the first try. Here's how to refine:

Refinement Prompts:

  • "Make it longer and more detailed" ‚Äî When content is too thin
  • "Add more specific examples" ‚Äî When it's too abstract
  • "Make the tone more conversational" ‚Äî When it sounds robotic
  • "Add step-by-step instructions for [X]" ‚Äî When it's missing actionability
  • "Include a checklist at the end" ‚Äî When you need action items
  • "Fix the [specific issue]" ‚Äî For targeted fixes

The key is to be specific. Don't say "make it better." Say exactly what you want changed.

Example Iteration:

You: "The lesson about [X] is good but I want you to add a real-world case study showing someone who implemented this and got results. Make it specific with numbers."

Claude will add exactly that. Be specific, get specific results.
